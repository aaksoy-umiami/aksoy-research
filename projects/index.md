My research focuses on advancing tropical cyclone prediction through data assimilation and examining the fundamental limits of atmospheric predictability.

## [Tropical Cyclone Data Assimilation](/projects/tropical-cyclone-da/)
Development of the HEDAS system and advanced vortex initialization techniques.

## [Radar Data Assimilation](/projects/radar-da/)
Collaborative work on assimilating high-resolution radar observations for convective storms.

## [Parameter Estimation](/projects/parameter-estimation/)
Techniques for simultaneously estimating atmospheric states and model parameters to reduce error.

## [Predictability & Dynamics](/projects/predictability/)
Theoretical work on error growth, chaos, and the limits of prediction using the Logistic Map.
